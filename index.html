Basic Spanish 1 Vocab

Spanish 1 Vocab

Names

Me llamo Adam - I call myself Adam.

Como te llamas? - What is your name?

Come se llamas? - What is his/her name?

Se llamas Adam - His name is Adam.

Y tu? - And you?

Greetings

Buenos dias - Good morning.

Buenas tardes - Good afternoon.

Buenos noches - Good night.

Mucho gusto - Nice to meet you.

Encantado(a) - Delighted to meet.)

Igualmente - Equally.

How Are You

Como estas? - How are you? (familiar).

Como esta usted? - How are you (formal).

Bien - Good.

Mas o menos - So so.

Mal - Bad.
